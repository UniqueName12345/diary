  • Ed Sheeran – Acoustic vibes and catchy hooks.
  • Flo Rida – Party anthems that never get old.

  • Post Malone – Genre-blending genius.

    Note: I listen sparingly because it's quite intense.
    Spotify | Bandcamp | YouTube
